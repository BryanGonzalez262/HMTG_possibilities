In this game, you will be observing one player, Wilson, as he interacts with multiple anonymous strangers...



Wilson has a ~*Magic Multiplying Box*~ that quadruples anything placed inside ...



In each interaction, a stranger, who cannot see Wilson through a wall, decides to put some amount of $10 into the ~*Magic Multiplying Box*~...



They do so, believing the ~*Magic Multiplying Box*~ will multiply their investment by 4 before it goes to Wilson ...



They expect Wilson to share the multiplied amount ...



→

In reality, however, the ~*Magic Multiplying Box*~ can multiply the amount by 2, 4, or 6 ...


Wilson is always aware of the stranger's expectation that the box multiplies by 4 ...


Your job, is to try and predict how much of the true multiplied amount Wilson will return to the stranger...


Afterward, you will be shown Wilson's decision, then watch him interact with someone new...


Sometimes, you will be asked to write down the reason for Wilson's decision...


Bonus!!


Remember to try learn about how Wilson is making decisions in order to more accurately predict his behavior.


At the end of the game, your accuracy on a randomly chosen trial will be used to compute a bonus!


The closer your guess is to Wilson’s actual decision, the larger your bonus will be.


Predict correctly, and you’ll receive an extra $2!

To Recap:

First: Others will give money for the magic box to quadruple.

Next: The box will yield less, more, or exactly what was expected, but only Wilson will know this!

Then: You’ll predict what Wilson will return.

Finally: You’ll find out how accurate your prediction was
